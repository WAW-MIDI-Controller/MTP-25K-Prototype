# Connecting to MIDI-OX

This page will take you through installing MIDI-OX, connecting the MTP-25K, and selecting a digital instrument.

## Installing MIDI-OX on a Windows PC

After downloading MIDI-OX from their [webpage](http://www.midiox.com/?http://www.midiox.com/moxdown.htm), launch midioxse.exe.

1. Click Setup.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX self extractor](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Self-Extractor.png)

2. Click Next.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX installer page 1](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Setup-Wizard-Page-1.png)

3. Select "I Agree" and click Next.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX installer page 1](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Setup-Wizard-Page-2.png)

4. Select "Personal Use" and click Next.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX installer page 1](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Setup-Wizard-Page-3.png)

5. Enter a UserName and click Next.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX installer page 1](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Setup-Wizard-Page-4.png)

6. Choose an installation location and click Next.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX installer page 1](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Setup-Wizard-Page-5.png)

7. Click Next and wait for the program to install.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX installer page 1](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Setup-Wizard-Page-6.png)

8. Close the installer.

## Setting Up MIDI-OX

1. Plug the MTP-25K into your computer.

2. Double-click the MIDI-OX icon on your desktop to launch the program.

![Screenshot of MIDI-OX desktop shortcut icon](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/Shortcut.png)

3. Open the MIDI Devices menu.

![Screenshot of icon for MIDI Device menu](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/MIDI-Devices-Icon.png)

4. Select the MTP-25K from the MIDI Inputs list. It will appear in the Port Map Objects list.

5. Select Microsoft GS Wavetable Synth from the MIDI Outpus list. It will appear in the Port Mapping list.

![Screenshot of MIDI Device menu](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/MIDI-Devices.png)

6. Click OK to close the MIDI Devices menu.

7. Open the MIDI Port Routing menu.

![Screenshot of icon for MIDI Port Routing menu](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/MIDI-Port-Routing-Icon.png)

8. If there is no connection between the MTP-25K and Microsoft GS Wavetable Synth, drag your mouse from one to the other.

![Screenshot of MIDI Port Routing menu](/Assets/Images/MIDIOX/MIDI-Port-Routing.png)
